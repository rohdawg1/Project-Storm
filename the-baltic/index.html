What We Do

Our Services

Crafted with care, made for you — discover what we have on offer.

Specialty Coffee

We source premium single-origin beans and craft each coffee with precision and care. From velvety flat whites to perfectly extracted espresso, every cup is made to order.

Brunch Menu

Our seasonal brunch showcases fresh local ingredients and creative flavour combinations. We offer generous portions and thoughtfully designed dishes that pair beautifully with our coffee.

Welcoming Atmosphere

The Baltic provides a relaxed, friendly space perfect for catching up with friends or settling in with a good book. Our team creates a genuine café culture that keeps customers coming back.

Quality Ingredients

We prioritise fresh, locally sourced produce and ethically obtained coffee beans whenever possible. Sustainability and quality are at the heart of everything we prepare.
