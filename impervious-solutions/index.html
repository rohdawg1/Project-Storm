Our Services

Professional, licensed, and reliable — the full range of services your property needs.

Basement Waterproofing

We seal cracks, install drainage systems, and apply protective membranes to keep basements dry year-round. Melbourne's high water table makes professional basement protection essential for long-term property protection.

Roof and Ceiling Leaks

Our specialists locate and repair roof leaks before they cause structural damage to your home or business. We use premium sealants and modern repair techniques trusted by Melbourne homeowners.

Commercial Waterproofing

Large-scale waterproofing projects for retail, office and industrial properties across Melbourne require precision and expertise. We minimise downtime and deliver results that protect your investment.

Moisture and Damp Treatment

We identify and eliminate moisture problems that lead to mould, odours and structural decay in older Melbourne properties. Early treatment prevents costly repairs and health hazards.
