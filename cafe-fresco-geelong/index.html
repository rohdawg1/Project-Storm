About Cafe Fresco Geelong

Cafe Fresco Geelong has been serving the local community with premium specialty coffee and thoughtfully prepared brunch since 2015. We're dedicated to sourcing quality ingredients and creating a welcoming space where locals and visitors alike can enjoy exceptional cafe culture.
